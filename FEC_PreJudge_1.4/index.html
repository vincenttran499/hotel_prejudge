Welcome to Srestub & Evad

We offer a restaurant, an arcade with a large amount of games for all ages to play! We have 3 rooms available to rent for parties and offer great party packages!


Srestub & Evad Dining

Srestub & Evad offers a multitude of food options that can vary from snacks to full course meals. Snacks are delicious and cheap for young kids to enjoy, Such as our Arcade Artichoke dip!

The Arcade

Our Arcade is filled with games of multiple genres! Old classics like "Dance Dance Revolution" or even new games of exciting shooters and race games are located here! Collect enough tickets and you will be able to access our wonderful prize reward area, filled with items like a gaming PC, a flat screen TV, and other top level prizes.

Event Rooms:

Our event rooms are very large, able to hold 75 people, and have certain packages that can be purchased with the event room for extra savings and fun for the whole group!
