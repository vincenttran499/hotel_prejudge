Welcome to the Arid Athens!

We offer a beautiful restaurant with a view of the area, Known as Dionysus' Garden. We have an Arid Athens exclusive, Pasithea's Pantheon; an area where guests from ages 12 and older may come in and relax.


Pasithea's Pantheon:

We offer a multitude of activities and sections for people to enjoy. Anyone 12 and older may come to the recreational center, which has a basketball court,other playground equipments, and other sport activities. Arid Athens also provides guests with a gym, a massage parlor, and our legendary Poseidon's Pool and Zeus's Spa.

Dionysus' Garden:

Dionysus' Garden is a 3 Star Michelin Guide Restaurant with a beautiful view of the Urban Jungle. It features a multitude of flavors across the world: Latin American, Italian, American, Asian, and European food for guests to choose from.

Other Services:

As a hotel and resort, we provide Valet Parking for all of our guests, Room Service, and Bell hops to assist you entering or leaving the Arid Athens.
